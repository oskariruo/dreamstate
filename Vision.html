Vision

Dreamstate is a collective of artists connected by a shared philosophy of manifestation and evolution. We work together by supporting eachothers individual artistry, and make sure that each member evolves in themselves as well as their art. This means that beyond all else we back the individuals growth and expansion firts and foremost before they may lay out their highest form. Consciousness is a central theme to Dreamstate as we believe that the quality of your consciousness directly correlates to the quality, purity and innovation in your work. And so we psuh each individual to expand their own awereness of themselves and the world around them to increase their level of consciousness and grow into better individuas as well as creators.
